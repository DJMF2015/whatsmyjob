Whats My Job? Android app

An occupational evaluation app for school leavers designed to help suggest potential career suggestions

The app uses an algorithm to produce a set of 'top ten jobs' and the 'top five' highest ranking work environments best suited to the user based upon their answers

Designed as project for MSc CS for Neuropsychologist UK, Stirling University 2016
